Nimda

Hitting the internet in 2001 Nimda becoming the fastest propagating computer virus at that time reaching the top of the list of reported attacks in 22 minutes.

Nimda was designed to attack Internet servers but could infect a home PC as well.

The virus created a backdoor into the victim's operating system and allowed the attacker to access the same level of functions as the current account running on the machine
