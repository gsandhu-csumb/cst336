Computer Viruses

Overview


Computer viruses are similar to real life viruses because of how they are designed to spread and infect one host to the next.

Computer viruses are malicious codes or programs written to change the way a computer works.

Melissa, Nimda, and Storm are viruses which have many differnt targets and victims but are transmitted commonly through servers and emails.
