Melissa

Created in 1991 by David L. Smith "Melissa" was a computer virus which spread through email.

Melissa was designed to tempt recipients into opening a document within an e-mail.

Once activated, the virus replicated itself and sent itself to the top 50 people in the recipient's e-mail address book
